Help Page

Home Page

The Home Page provides an overview of your daily nutrition goals and progress. Here you can view your daily nutritional summaries, recent meals, and quick access to frequently used features.

Food Management

Food Diary

Track your daily meals and snacks in the Food Diary. Add foods, specify portions, and monitor your nutritional intake throughout the day. The diary automatically calculates your daily totals for calories, protein, carbs, and fats.

Recipe Search

Find new recipes based on your preferences and dietary requirements. Save your favorite recipes for quick access and add them directly to your meal plan.

Meal Plan

Create weekly meal plans to organize your meals in advance. This helps with grocery shopping and maintaining consistent nutrition goals.

Grocery Management

Grocery List

Generate shopping lists based on your meal plans. Add, remove, or check off items as you shop. The list automatically organizes items by category for easier shopping.

Grocery Search

Search for grocery items and add them to your shopping list. You can also view nutritional information for grocery items to make informed choices.

Nutrition Tracking

Daily Overview

View your daily nutrition totals and compare them against your goals. Track calories, protein, carbohydrates, and fats throughout the day.

Nutrition History

Access your nutrition history to view trends and averages over time. See your progress over 7, 14, 30, or 90 days to help maintain your nutrition goals.

Getting Started

1. Create an Account or Login: Start by creating an account or logging in to access all features.

2. Set Your Nutrition Goals: Navigate to the Nutrition section to set your daily goals for calories, protein, carbs, and fats.

3. Start Your Food Diary: Begin logging your meals in the Food Diary to track your nutrition intake.

4. Create a Meal Plan: Use the Meal Plan feature to organize your meals for the week.

5. Generate Grocery Lists: Based on your meal plan, create shopping lists in the Grocery section.

6. Track Your Progress: Monitor your nutrition history to ensure you're meeting your goals.

Tips for Success

  • Log your meals regularly to maintain accurate nutrition tracking
  • Plan your meals in advance to make grocery shopping easier
  • Review your nutrition history weekly to stay on track with your goals
  • Update your goals as needed based on your progress
  • Use the recipe search to add variety to your meal plans
